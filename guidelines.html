UniSportHub Sports Management System

Rules and Guidelines

1. User Registration and Accounts

  • Accurate Information: Provide accurate and up-to-date information during registration.
  • One Account per User: Each user is allowed only one account. Sharing accounts is prohibited.
  • Account Security: Keep your login credentials secure. Do not share your password with others.
  • Inactive Accounts: Inactive accounts may be deactivated after 6 months of no activity.

2. Sports Event Participation

  • Eligibility: Only registered students and staff with valid accounts are eligible to participate in events.
  • Fair Play: All participants must adhere to the principles of fair play and sportsmanship.
  • Code of Conduct: Abusive, discriminatory, or unsportsmanlike behavior will not be tolerated.
  • Injury Reporting: Report any injuries sustained during events to the event organizer or medical staff immediately.

3. Team Formation and Management

  • Team Registration: Teams must be registered before the event deadline.
  • Captain Responsibilities: Team captains must ensure team compliance with rules.
  • Substitutions: Only approved substitutions are allowed during matches.

4. Match Scheduling and Attendance

  • Match Timings: Participants must adhere to the match schedule.
  • Rescheduling Requests: Must be made 48 hours in advance.
  • Walkovers: A team forfeits if not present within 15 minutes of match time.

5. Equipment and Attire

  • Proper Gear: Approved equipment must be used.
  • Safety Gear: Required protective equipment must be worn.
  • Equipment Maintenance: Borrowed equipment must be returned in good condition.

6. Disciplinary Actions and Penalties

  • Misconduct Penalties: Players may face suspension or disqualification for violations.
  • Referee Authority: Official decisions are final.
  • Appeals: Appeals must be backed by valid evidence.

7. Facility Usage and Maintenance

  • Respect for Facilities: Cleanliness and proper usage are required.
  • Booking Regulations: Facilities must be booked in advance.
  • Liability: UniSportHub is not responsible for lost belongings.

8. Amendments to Rules

UniSportHub reserves the right to update or modify these rules and guidelines at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes.

By using the UniSportHub Sports Management System, you agree to abide by these rules and guidelines. Thank you for your cooperation!
